Courses

AJLM has designed the Branding & Growth Acceleration (B.G.A) Programme, to ignite your vision as a jewellery & luxury professional. Curated by a dedicated team of experts excelling in their career, the mentors will be sharing their knowledge, ideologies and experience which can be directly applied to your businesses. This course helps you strategise and turn your branding plan into action, keeping in mind the spectrum of the resources available to you.


The B.G.A Programme is for you if you are:

  • A jewellery entrepreneur looking at innovative ways to grow your customer base.
  • A solo business woman.
  • A one man army led enterprise.
  • Currently depending only on word of mouth for your business.
  • A young jewellery professional interested in adding another feather to your CV.
  • A jewellery & luxury enterprise who wants to learn the nuances of new age digital marketing, branding and PR strategies.
