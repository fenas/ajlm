About Us

A Sneak Peak into, what makes us different!!

AJLM as an academy envisions to magnify your creative outlook and enhance your understanding of new age as well as traditional branding mantras. We are a one stop programme imparting all the essential fundamentals that could put you on the roadmap of a flourishing global brand. Keeping in focus the needs of the jewellery & luxury segment, AJLM is a one of it's kind initiative designed to encourage entrepreneurs, solo-business women, and designers from around the country. Our mission is to propel success through exchange of ingenious ideas and applied knowledge.

Building values to shape the future of your brand!
