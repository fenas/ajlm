About Us

The AJLM curriculum covers the main components of designing a successful jewellery business. From the art of forecasting to the strategies of marketing, our course helps self-taught jewellery makers, industry professionals, traders and jewellery enthusiasts grow their business.

Valuable Business Skills

AJLM is an industry-wide initiative to help entrepreneurs, solo-business women, and jewellery makers from around the country to develop a successful brand. Through decades of industry experience, the project is designed to offer a community experience to encourage success through collaboration. You will be walking away with valuable business skills that will help empower you to take your business to the next level. Our mission is to empower emerging and semi-established jewellery professionals to have confidence in their business and grow their profession with the right knowledge in the domain.
