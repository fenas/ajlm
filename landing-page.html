  • About us

  • The AJLM curriculum covers the main components of designing a successful jewellery business. From the art of forecasting to the strategies of marketing, our course helps self-taught jewellery makers, industry professionals, traders and jewellery enthusiasts grow their business.

    AJLM is an industry-wide initiative to help entrepreneurs, solo-business women, and jewellery makers from around the country to develop a successful brand. Through decades of industry experience, the project is designed to offer a community experience to encourage success through collaboration.

    You will be walking away with valuable business skills that will help empower you to take your business to the next level. Our mission is to empower emerging and semi-established jewellery professionals to have confidence in their business and grow their profession with the right knowledge in the domain.

  • Course Details

    The course is structured to provide professionals a thorough understanding of the design, business and operational side of luxury jewellery making. The workshop is designed to deliver maximum value to jewellery professionals that are committed to growing their business.

    Core concepts such as segmentation, differentiation, branding and merchandising, will be taught to entrepreneurs that want to excel in the evolving luxury jewellery market. We have shortlisted 10 essential knowledge tools that every jewellery maker needs to have when growing their business.

    By learning from industry leaders in the domain, you can gain valuable insights that will impact your business throughout its lifetime. The course is designed to offer premier coaching and advanced networking opportunities to all participants.

    • SOCIAL MEDIA PRESENCE

      Creating a robust social media presence is the key to succeeding in the luxury jewellery making space in 2019. Our expert, Ami Savla, will demonstrate the best strategies to master Instagram, Pinterest & Facebook. Areas such as influencer marketing, cross branding, online marketing and campaigning will be taught in a collaborative format.

    • EVALUATING YOUR BUSINESS POSITION

      Tarveen Kaur will take all participants through the comprehensive process of analysing your business proposition. The workshop will cover key areas such as creating your brand story, image branding, social skill development and brand management.

    • VISUAL MERCHANDISING

      Visual merchandising is one of the most important aspects of our industry. The workshop will teach emerging professionals the best strategies to create growth through effective visual merchandising. Taught by Sonali Tribhuvan, an ace design consultant & professor, who has studied design at Central Saint Martins, London.

    • FORECASTING

      By obtaining the right skills in forecasting, jewellery professionals can stay competitive in the market and create customer-focused jewellery. Forecasting, using the right metrics and indicators, can revolutionize the way that your business operates.

    • E-COMMERCE & MARKETPLACE

      The luxury jewellery space is shifting online, with more outlets being available for emerging and semi-established professionals. The e-commerce domain is a premier example of digital leadership, as more buyers go online to find their preferred jewellery pieces.

    • JEWELLERY PHOTOGRAPHY

      Taking the right picture can make a world of a difference when it comes to the jewellery business. Your pieces come alive and become that much more attractive upon first glance. Our expert, Preeta Agarwal, will take you through the basics of photography in this masterclass.

    • PR

      While traditional and digital media have evolved, more jewellery professionals need to reach out to them directly. This workshop by Preeta Agarwal is designed to impart the right strategies about how to approach journalists, and have your brand be in front of millions of buyers across the country.

    • VERTICALS IN JEWELLERY

      The industry has a wide range of career options, ready to be explored. Our workshop is designed to help you find the right approach, leveraging your skillsets across core industry segments.

    • DESIGN INNOVATION

      Leading with design innovation is the key to staying ahead of the market. Emerging professionals can make unique pieces that stand out from the competition. Having the right approach towards innovation can make your business that much more successful.

    • TRADE SHOW STRATEGIES

      Trade shows can infuse new lines of opportunities for your business. We will showcase the right ways to create trade show success, so that you can make the most from your experience. The leading trade shows happen throughout the year, for which you will have to prepare in advance.
